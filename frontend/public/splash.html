✨ v1.1 update

New ways to sign in

Don't love Google? You now have three ways to sign in — and your private items are easier to find.

  • Email + password, magic link, or Google — pick any from the /signin page. Same account, your choice.
  • Profile editor with per-field privacy: each optional detail (avatar, contact link, location) has its own Public / Private toggle. Off by default.
  • Find your private items via the new Public / Mine filter on the Gallery's Designs & Components tabs.
  • AI fixes — Sculpture style works again, and a transient hiccup no longer drops your in-flight generation.

Hit ? in the workspace and check the Account & Sign-in section for the full rundown.
