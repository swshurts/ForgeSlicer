Welcome to ForgeSlicer

A browser-based 3D CAD + slicer built for makers — no installs, no fuss.

  • Design with primitives, booleans, fillet & chamfer, and OrcaSlicer-style cut.
  • Speak commands hands-free with Whisper + GPT.
  • Generate meshes from text or images via Meshy AI.
  • Share designs & components to the public gallery — earn the Contributor for Life badge for unlimited free access.
